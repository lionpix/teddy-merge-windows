## 2. About / Description Section
*A slightly deeper dive into what the application is and who it is for.*

**Title:** Simplify Your Document Management
**Content:** 
Teddy Merge is a highly efficient, user-friendly utility designed to easily combine multiple PDF documents and images into a single, organized PDF file. Whether you are managing personal records, assembling a professional report, or organizing a portfolio, Teddy Merge streamlines the process with an intuitive drag-and-drop interface.

You can effortlessly rearrange items, add files on the fly, and instantly export a polished, merged document in seconds.

## 3. Key Features Section
*Best displayed as a grid of icons or a bulleted list.*

*   **Privacy First:** All processing happens entirely locally on your Windows machine. Your sensitive files and data are never uploaded to the cloud or shared.
*   **Simplicity at its Core:** No complicated menus or unnecessary settings. Just add your files, arrange them, and merge.
*   **Versatile Input:** Supports combining both existing PDF documents and image files (JPG, PNG) into a unified PDF format.
*   **Lightning Fast:** Built natively for Windows utilizing modern WinUI 3, ensuring optimal performance and a seamless experience.
*   **Visual Reordering:** An intuitive drag-and-drop interface for quick visual reordering of your pages before export.

**Effective Date:** March 2026

**Overview**
Lion Pixel ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and security. This Privacy Policy outlines how the Teddy Merge application ("the Application") handles your information.

**Data Collection and Usage**
Teddy Merge is completely offline, on-device utility software. 
*   **No Personal Data Collection:** The Application does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data, user accounts, telemetry, or analytics.
